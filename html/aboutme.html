About Me

My interest in voice work started from childhood, when my friends and I would attempt to mimic various Pok√©mon characters from the anime. I'm sure it was aggravating to any nearby adults, but it was quite fun!

Since then I've dreamed of pointing out a character on screen to my friends and waggling my eyebrows at them, saying, "Recognise that voice?" and them responding, "...No..."
To me this indicates a job well done, and a future prank in the making!

I am a lifelong enjoyer of all video games, large and small - the only exception being horror games in which I cannot fight back. Seriously, give me a single near-useless bullet and I'll give it a shot!

I've completed several hundred games at this point and have been on a few world leaderboards, and I'm just getting started! Stop by my channels if you're in the mood for some gameplay!

I'm always learning new things every day. If I'm not the perfect fit for your project right now, you can bet I'll be working to become a better fit down the road. I am happy to mould myself into the right fit for your project!

Feel free to explore my demos or reach out - I'd love to bring your characters to life!
